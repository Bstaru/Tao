TAO

Our game belongs to the genre First Person Shooter and will handle the steampunk style, based on Chinese culture giving it a cartoon touch.
The player will take the role of our character, who has the name of Tao, who is a soldier who was assigned the mission of stealing the plans and strategies of his enemies.
For this he had to infiltrate one of the enemy bases that are full of guards machines that watch and take care of the base both from outside and inside.
The player must walk the stage and avoid the guards machines to see him or, failing that, fight with them to achieve his goal, which is the palace.
